Class Details

A practical option for Wattala students who want MMA and fitness together.

This class is designed for students who want combat-sport fundamentals and overall conditioning in the same session. It is suitable for adults and committed beginners who want steady progress.

  • Every Sunday from 3.30pm to 5.30pm.
  • Every Tuesday from 6.30pm to 8.30pm.
  • MMA COLOMBO, Wattala.
  • MMA with fitness-focused training.
  • Confirm availability before attending: +94 75 341 4414.
Ideal For

Beginners and developing athletes

Start with guided technique, controlled conditioning and steady progression without needing previous fight experience.

Training Focus

Technique plus conditioning

Sessions blend movement, striking basics, combat fitness and practical discipline-building work.

What To Bring

Come ready to move

Comfortable training clothes, water and a focused attitude are enough for an enquiry or first class discussion.
