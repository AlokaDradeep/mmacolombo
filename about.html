Who We Are

More than 25 years in martial arts development and coaching.

Chief Instructor Master N.S. Kumara has explored martial arts since 1989, learning, teaching and refining techniques across multiple systems to create accurate, practical training for real-world results.

MMA Colombo supports beginners, recreational students and serious fighters with a balanced system of technical instruction, fitness training and progressive skill development.

This club is registered as a sports club of the Western Provincial Council, Sri Lanka.

  • MMA and striking fundamentals
  • Fitness and conditioning
  • Self-defense and tactics
  • Kids and adult coaching
