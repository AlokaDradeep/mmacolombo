About Us

About MMA COLOMBO

MMA Colombo brings more than 25 years of martial arts practice, development and coaching into one disciplined training system focused on effective, practical results.

Chief Instructor Master N.S. Kumara has explored martial arts since 1989, creating a club that supports children, youth and adults from beginner level to advanced training.

This club is registered as a sports club of the Western Provincial Council, Sri Lanka.

About RFC

RFC (Real Fighting Combat System)

True fighting is not a competition but a challenge. It cannot be limited by rules and regulations when you are protecting your life and body. The response time is short, so you need to act effectively and exit safely before the situation becomes more serious.

For this reason, target attack training, tactical invasion, tactical evacuation, strategic intelligence and tactical retreat work are practiced as part of the RFC Real Fighting Combat System.

Progression Path

GRADINGS AND BELT LINEAGE

At MMA Colombo, students can grow through a structured grading system from white belt to black belt while also taking part in recognized kickboxing and jiu-jitsu belt lineage pathways.

Club Gradings

White belt to black belt development

Our club supports step-by-step progress through technical skill, discipline, fitness and practical understanding so students can advance with clear goals and earned results.

  • Structured student progression from beginner level to black belt standard
  • Technical review, discipline, consistency and coaching guidance
  • Suitable for children, youth and adults who train with commitment
Kickboxing Pathway

Participation in kickboxing belt lineage

Students who focus on striking can build toward kickboxing progression opportunities through regular training, skill development and participation in the appropriate grading path.

  • Develop striking fundamentals, combinations and ring discipline
  • Progress with coach guidance through practical kickboxing training
  • Supports students interested in recognition within kickboxing lineage
Jiu-Jitsu Pathway

Participation in jiu-jitsu belt lineage

Students who want grappling development can also take part in jiu-jitsu belt lineage pathways, building control, technical awareness and long-term progression.

  • Encourages steady development in grappling and technical control
  • Connects student progress with recognized jiu-jitsu belt advancement
  • Gives committed students another route for measurable achievement
